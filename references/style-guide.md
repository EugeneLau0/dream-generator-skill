# Dream Style Guide

Use these profiles to shape imagery, pacing, and emotional temperature. Blend styles when the user asks for a mixed tone.

## Gentle Healing

- Mood: quiet, forgiving, soft strangeness.
- Images: warm windows, rain after heat, clean rooms, distant singing, animals at a safe distance, morning light.
- Motion: slow transitions, recognition, return, release.
- Ending: a small object, phrase, or warmth remains without explaining whether the scene has ended.

## Eerie Suspense

- Mood: unsettled but controlled.
- Images: empty stations, repeated doors, wrong reflections, familiar voices from impossible places, lights that hum.
- Motion: pursuit without explicit violence, spaces looping, names disappearing.
- Ending: reveal one quiet impossibility instead of a shock.

## Fairytale Fable

- Mood: symbolic, simple surface with deep undertow.
- Images: talking objects, forests, wells, impossible bargains, small keys, birds, moons, old houses.
- Motion: three-part recurrence, rule, bargain, transformation.
- Ending: wonder or consequence, not explanation.

## Cyber Neon

- Mood: bright, alien, lonely, synthetic.
- Images: glass rain, vending machines, data temples, stations under artificial dawn, screens that dream back.
- Motion: identity glitch, memory upload, city behaving like a body.
- Ending: human sensation surviving inside machinery.

## Old Memory

- Mood: nostalgic, tender, slightly unreliable.
- Images: childhood rooms, school corridors, summer dust, old calls, missing relatives, changed streets.
- Motion: returning to a place that no longer matches itself.
- Ending: an impossible reunion, half-remembered sentence, or waking with a lost smell.

## Absurd Dark Humor

- Mood: deadpan, strange, comic unease.
- Images: bureaucratic dreams, talking appliances, ceremonial inconveniences, impossible forms to fill.
- Motion: everyone accepts the impossible as normal.
- Ending: a mundane detail becomes the strangest thing.

## Lonely Poetic

- Mood: spacious, quiet, emotionally suspended.
- Images: water, snow, empty rooms, trains, far lights, unread letters, sky inside buildings.
- Motion: drifting, searching, almost meeting.
- Ending: absence becomes tactile.

## Epic Fantasy

- Mood: grand, mythic, luminous, but still dreamlike.
- Images: towers, ancient bridges, sleeping cities, stars beneath lakes, animals made of weather, broken crowns.
- Motion: quest logic, omen, threshold, transformation.
- Ending: the narrator is chosen, forgotten, or left carrying a sign.
